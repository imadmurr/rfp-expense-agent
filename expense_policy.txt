Javista Services SAL - RFP Expense Policy
==========================================
Version 2.0 | Effective: January 2026

1. CONSULTANT RATE CAPS
   - Senior Developers: Maximum $110/hour
   - Junior Developers: Maximum $65/hour
   - AI Engineers: Maximum $150/hour
   - Project Managers: Maximum $105/hour
   - QA Testers: Maximum $55/hour
   - DevOps Engineers: Maximum $105/hour
   - UX Designers: Maximum $80/hour
   - Business Analysts: Maximum $85/hour
   - Technical Writers: Maximum $55/hour

2. TRAVEL EXPENSES
   - Maximum travel budget per project: $5,000
   - Per diem allowance: $75/day
   - Flight bookings must be economy class for domestic, business class for international
   - Hotel maximum: $150/night

3. SOFTWARE & LICENSES
   - Pre-approved licenses do not require additional approval
   - Licenses exceeding $2,000 require manager approval
   - Annual license renewals must be budgeted in Q4

4. TRAINING & MATERIALS
   - Maximum training budget per consultant: $500
   - Certification exam fees are fully reimbursable
   - Conference attendance requires pre-approval

5. EQUIPMENT
   - Equipment purchases over $1,000 require CTO approval
   - Laptops are provided by Javista (not billable to client)
   - Specialized hardware may be billed with client pre-approval

6. RFP SUBMISSION RULES
   - All RFP responses must include a minimum 10% profit margin
   - Discount offers exceeding 15% require CEO approval
   - Payment terms: Net 30 days standard, Net 60 requires CFO approval
   - Proposals must be submitted at least 48 hours before deadline

7. APPROVAL THRESHOLDS
   - Under $10,000: Team Lead approval
   - $10,000 - $50,000: Director approval
   - Over $50,000: CTO + CEO approval
